How to upload images?

The uploading of images directly to the Openthings server is not possible yet. This page will descibe how you still can add images to your project description.

Step 1:

To upload an image and add it to your projects description you first have to upload your images to a website such as: tinypic.

Step 2:

Collect the image links of your uploaded images and paste them in your detailed description field.

Step 3:

Add an exclamation mark at the start and end of your links so it will look like this: !http://i62.tinypic.com/6nv6ug.jpg!

Step 4:

Save! Your images should no be visable in the description
